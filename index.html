Syracuse to a Tea

Not your average tea party.

Syracuse to a Tea is the CNY community for all things tea-related. We welcome tea lovers at all levels and from all backgrounds.

Join us every 2nd Wednesday of the month 5:30-7:30pm for our member meetup!

"I love drinking tea, and I love that we do more than that here. I've discovered new events, shops, and attractions in the area thanks to Syracuse to a Tea, and I've lived here all my life!" - Jade Tortellini

"The tea is great and the people I've met are even better. You'd be hard-pressed to find a cooler place to talk about the Tang Dynasty, aliens and streudel recipes- all in the same day!" - Guy Whoscool
